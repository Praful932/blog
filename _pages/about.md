Currently working as a Senior ML Engineer at [Visa](https://visa.com/) on the AI Governance team, building *AI Observatory* - an internal platform to monitor and govern ML models, GenAI applications, and agents across Visa. [Read](https://fortune.com/2025/10/07/visa-artificial-intelligence-science-art/) about what it can do.

Most recently I worked on determining [effect of quantization on VLMs](https://praful932.com/blog-5-latentlens-quant-exp/) and on reproducing a method to [predict antibody thermal stability](https://github.com/Praful932/antibody-developability-abmelt). My Areas of Interest - LLM Interpretability (how information is encoded across layers : my current work examines the effect of quantization on visual-token interpretability), how a model's internal beliefs shift as context accumulates, decoding methods, AI safety (lie detection, abliteration) and AI in healthcare. At present I'm working towards a research career (with a good intersection in engineering).

Prior to Visa, I spent ~4 years at [Prodigal](https://www.prodigaltech.com/) (YC-backed) as an ML Engineer, where I owned the ML engine for a call summarization product generating ~100k summaries/day, finetuned LLMs for conversation understanding, and built NER models for healthcare PII redaction.

Before that I was an undergrad ('22) 🎓 at [DYPCOE](https://www.dypcoeakurdi.ac.in/) studying Information Technology. During that time I collaborated with [Omdena](https://omdena.com/) on AI projects with startups in [healthcare](https://rebootrx.org/) and [energy](https://youtu.be/MnFkPazZULU), and interned at [BimaPe (now Verak)](https://www.linkedin.com/company/verak/) working on information extraction. I also shipped a few [open-source projects](https://github.com/Praful932) with great collaborators along the way.
